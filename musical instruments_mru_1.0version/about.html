🎵 About Indian Musical Instruments

← Back to Instruments

India has a rich tradition of classical music with a wide variety of string, wind, and percussion instruments. These instruments have been passed down over centuries and are deeply rooted in regional cultures and devotional practices.

Instruments like the Veena, Sitar, Sarangi, Santoor, and Flute are not only used in classical concerts but are also central to storytelling, folklore, and spiritual rituals. The Shenoy (Shehnai), for example, is often played at weddings and temple ceremonies.

This chart-style project celebrates the beauty of these instruments and aims to preserve their knowledge using Bharati Script, so young learners and enthusiasts across India can access this cultural heritage in a unified script.
